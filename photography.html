About Me

Hello, my name is Alina Yildir. I am a photographer based in Ottawa, Canada.

I have been professionally shooting for 5 years; over the past few years, I am an official photographer for American Wedding Group.

About Wedding Photography

My style is documentary and candid; I try to limit the number of posed or staged photos (unless specifically requested). Although I do shoot group photos and I always take the couple away for a romantic photo shoot, I do not try to influence the day or create moments that are not real, I love capturing the real story of a wedding.

I pay a lot of attention to the details to ensure that all elements within the photo - the lighting, the composition, the subject, and everything else in between - convey the right vision or message.

I look forward to hearing from you.
